The Gate

[1,0] You have entered the pit. The height of the ceiling and slick stone will not allow you back out. This is a cave made by nature, but lived in by something else. There is a rough floor. Cave

Greatroom

Huge fireplaces lie to the north and south, filled with long dead embers. A wooden door fitted with a lock lies to the north.

[2,1] This is a round domed room of carved stone about the length and width of a house, and as high. The surfaces are all made of fist sized stones that fit together tightly. The Well

[1,2] This room is round, about twenty steps in diameter and has a well at the center with a chain pulley and large bucket. The air is very damp here and moisture beads on the walls. Empty hooks for lamps are seen on two sides. The exit doorway lies to the south.

The Crypt

[2,2] This is a room with a curved ceiling. It is shaped like a very wide hallway, and has the same stone structure as the outside. The air here is stale and dense. You see some movment to the north, and are now face to face with a shambaling corpse. You must fight to survive! The exit lies to the south.
